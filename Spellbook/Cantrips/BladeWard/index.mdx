Source: Player's Handbook

Abjuration cantrip

Casting Time: 1 action 
Range: Self
Components: V, S
Duration: 1 round

You extend your hand and trace a sigil of warding in the air. 
Until the end of your next turn, you have resistance against bludgeoning, piercing, 
and slashing damage dealt by weapon attacks.

Spell Lists. Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
